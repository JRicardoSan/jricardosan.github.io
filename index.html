J Ricardo Sanchez Ibanez

GNC and Robotics Engineer

Airbus Defence and Space

My name is Ricardo, but you can call me Ric. During the last years, I have devoted my career and skills to the space robotics field, contributing to improve the autonomous capabilities of rovers. These systems are nowadays the only solution to transport scientific instruments to other planets and to move on their surface. This is why they are key to continue expanding our knowledge about the universe and to unveil its secrets.

I hold a PhD in Mechatronics from the University of Malaga (UMA), in Spain. I have been in the UMA Space Robotics Laboratory since its creation (btw, I designed the logo :D). Besides, I carried out three research stays between 2017 and 2019 in the Automation and Robotics group at ESA-ESTEC, with a total duration of one year. I also did a one-month research visit to the DFKI Robotics and Innovation Center in Bremen, Germany. I was a Research and Development Specialist at the SpaceR research group at the University of Luxembourg in 2021.

To find a record of my publications please check my Google Scholar profile.

Have a nice day!

Drawing robotic souls, Ric
